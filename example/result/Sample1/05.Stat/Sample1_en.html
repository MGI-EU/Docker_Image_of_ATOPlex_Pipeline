1. Sample: Sample name;

2. Clean_Reads: The number of clean FASTQ;

3. GAPDH_Reads: The number of GAPDH reads;

4. Lambda_Reads: The number of Lambda reads;

5. SARS-CoV-2_Reads: The reads number of identified SARS-CoV-2;

6. GAPDH_Rate: The proportion of GAPDH reads: 100%*(GAPDH reads number)/(clean reads number);

7. Lambda_Rate: The proportion of Lambda reads: 100%*(Lambda reads number)/(clean reads number);

8. SARS-CoV-2_Reads_Pct: The proportion of SARS-CoV-2 reads: 100%*(SARS-CoV-2 reads number)/((lambda DNA reads number)+(SARS-CoV-2 reads number));

9. Genome_Average_Depth: Average depth of whole SARS-CoV-2 genome;

10. ≥1X_Region_Average_Depth: Average depth of SARS-CoV-2 genome region where the depth is greater than or equal to 1X;

11. ≥1X_Coverage: The coverage is calculated as proportion of reference genome covered by more than 1 folds reads;

12. ≥1X_size: The length of SARS-CoV-2 genome region where the depth is greater than or equal to 1X;

13. ≥100X_Coverage: The coverage is calculated as proportion of reference genome covered by more than 100 folds reads;

14. ≥100X_size: The length of SARS-CoV-2 genome region where the depth is greater than or equal to 100X;

15. Identification_Result: Sample identification result according to SARS-CoV-2_Reads_Pct: Positive(≥0.1%), Indetermination(0.05%-0.1%), Negative(<0.05%);
